Fundamentals

Learn essential email concepts in just 5 minutes.

📄️ Account

In RustMail, an account is a one-to-one mapping to an email address (e.g., user@gmail.com) from any mail service you want to sync. This could be a Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, or any other email account supporting IMAP and SMTP.

📄️ Mailbox

A mailbox is a storage area on an email server where emails are kept, organized, and managed. It’s assigned to a user or a specific purpose (e.g., "Inbox"). Think of it as a digital filing cabinet for your emails.
